About qTalk

Nowadays, every government seems to be interested in what we're saying online. qTalk is built on a "privacy goes first" agenda, and we make no compromises. Your safety is our top priority, and there isn't anything in the world that will change that.

Security

qTalk takes your privacy seriously.
With leading-class encryption, you can rest assured knowing that the only people reading your messages are the ones you send them to.

Ease of Use

Unlike other secure messaging solutions, qTalk does not require you to be a computer programmer to use it. qTalk comes out-of-the-box with an easy-to-use interface that allows you to focus on your conversations.

Freedom

qTalk is both free for you to use, and free for you to change. You are completely free to both use and modify qTalk. Furthermore, qTalk will never harass you with ads, or require you to pay for features.
